## 🎯 The Problem

In emergency situations, official services (police / ambulance / 112) often respond slowly due to call queues, IVR delays, and distance. Meanwhile, nearby people who could help remain completely unaware. Existing safety apps either:

- ❌ Compromise user privacy through continuous location tracking, **or**
- ❌ Fail to build a trustworthy community response due to misuse and false alerts.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

**ResQNow** turns every nearby smartphone into a potential lifeline. With one tap, gesture, or voice command, your alert reaches verified helpers within seconds — while keeping you in full control of your data.

### 🌟 Core Features

- 🆘 **One-Tap / Gesture SOS** — panic-friendly trigger, no menus
- 📍 **Smart Nearby Alert** — radius-based community activation (configurable, e.g. 500 m)
- 🗺️ **Real-Time Live Tracking** — bi-directional victim ↔ helper map
- 🛡️ **Track-Me Mode** — pre-emergency proactive safety sharing
- 🤖 **AI Safety Layer** — Gemini-powered guidance & risk detection
- ⭐ **Reputation System** — trust score prevents misuse
- 🚩 **In-App Reporting** — false-alert and abuse handling
- 🎥 **Video Safety Filter** — AI screens unsafe / triggering content
- 🗣️ **Multilingual Voice SOS** — works for women, teens, and elderly
- 📡 **Offline SMS Fallback** — works in low-connectivity zones

### 🔄 Three Modes

| Mode | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **PANIC** | True emergencies — instant alert to nearby helpers + emergency contacts |
| **HELP** | Non-critical assistance — community-based help requests |
| **TRACK-ME** | Pre-emergency proactive safety sharing (e.g. walking home late) |
